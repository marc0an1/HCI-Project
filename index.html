SWIFTBAT Airways Overview

About SWIFTBAT Airways

SWIFTBAT Airways is a modern airline known for delivering exceptional service and innovative travel experiences, with a focus on destinations across North and South America, as well as select routes to Europe.

Destinations

SWIFTBAT connects travelers to over 120 cities in the U.S., the Caribbean, Latin America, and key European destinations.

Hubs

SWIFTBAT's primary hub is Miami International Airport in Florida, with secondary hubs in Los Angeles and Atlanta.

Flights

SWIFTBAT operates more than 900 flights daily, ensuring frequent and flexible travel options.

Services

Passengers enjoy complimentary gourmet snacks and beverages, free high-speed Wi-Fi on all flights, and access to a broad selection of live TV channels and in-flight entertainment options.

Legroom

SWIFTBAT offers some of the most spacious legroom in its economy class, prioritizing passenger comfort.

Fare Options

SWIFTBAT provides flexible fare options that include premium seat selection, priority boarding, and loyalty rewards points.

Change and Cancel

SWIFTBAT offers a hassle-free policy that allows customers to change or cancel their travel plans without additional fees in most cases.

Mission and Values

SWIFTBAT Airways was founded in 2024 in Miami, Florida, with a mission to revolutionize air travel. SwiftBat’s core values—innovation, comfort, safety, transparency, and sustainability—drive its commitment to offering a superior flying experience while reducing environmental impact.
